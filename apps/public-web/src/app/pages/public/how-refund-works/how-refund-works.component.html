Success looks different for different people.

Instead of one rule, we built a fair system for all.

WORKING PROFESSIONALS

Refund triggers if they achieve ANY one:

  • Salary jump
  • Switch to better company
  • Freelance project
  • Promotion / responsibility upgrade
  • Deliver a real product inside their company

Professionals don’t need motivation,
they need growth and confidence.

COLLEGE STUDENTS / GRADUATES

Refund triggers if they achieve ANY one:

  • Get hired or internship
  • Freelance client
  • Paid project
  • Build a real product with users
  • Enter company with portfolio advantage

Students can’t say:
“I am still learning.” They must execute.

SCHOOL STUDENTS (YES, THEY CAN JOIN)

Refund triggers if they achieve ANY one:

  • Paid freelance project
  • Product with real users
  • Small startup / service
  • Client website / app delivery
  • Build something with traction

Because if a school kid can ship real work,
they deserve reward, not just applause.

DROPOUTS / CAREER SWITCHERS

Refund triggers if they achieve ANY one:

  • Earn from skill
  • Get job, internship or contract
  • Build a real product with users
  • Start a small service-based business

We don’t care about degrees.
We care about output.

ENTREPRENEURS / STARTUP BUILDERS

Refund triggers if they achieve ANY one:

  • Launch MVP with real users
  • Onboard first paying customer
  • Generate revenue
  • Deliver tech/design for a real client

If you build something that works in the real world,
your fee comes back.

This is not an education business.
This is a performance-based talent pipeline.

Why this system is powerful

No one can say “ProPirates took my money and gave nothing.”
Lazy people automatically filter out.
Serious people trust the system.
The accelerator grows with real success stories.
Only strong performers get rewarded.

After 60 days, refunds aren’t guaranteed, but support continues.
Proof required: offer letter, paid invoice, client confirmation, contract, company confirmation, or verifiable live product revenue.

Have any questions?

Simple, strict, and fair. We don’t select everyone — we select doers who can work under pressure, learn fast, and take responsibility for their output.
